ENGI1006 Assignments

Homework 1:

Homework 1 was all about getting the basics of programming down. The written section taught the basics of binary and algorithms. THe programming taught list basics.

Homework 2:

The written part of homework 2 went over sorting and searching. The programming part was a physics simulation, which introduced us to matplotlib. We also began the machine learning library.

Homework 3:

The written part of homework 4 went over sets, dictionaries, and recursion. The programming part of it was percolation, which thoroughly taught recursion, and we kept building the machine learning library

Homework 4:

Homework 4 was out introduction to Object Oriented Programming in Python. We made a model class with students and a teacher.

Homework 5:

Homework 5 was the culmination of our machine learning library. We were introduced to many powerful resources such as pandas, seaborn, and scikit-learn.

My favorite assignment by far was Homework 5. The introduction to these powerful libraries was stellar. The power in these are nothing short of awesome, and it makes me excited for when I use them again.
